HOMEOWNERS INSURANCE POLICY
Policy Number: HOME-2024-155
Effective Date: March 15, 2024

DWELLING COVERAGE (Coverage A)
Limit: $350,000
Covers the physical structure of your home including:
- Walls, roof, floors, and foundation
- Attached structures (garage, deck)
- Built-in appliances
- Plumbing, heating, and electrical systems

Replacement cost coverage - no depreciation applied.

OTHER STRUCTURES (Coverage B)
Limit: $35,000 (10% of Coverage A)
Covers detached structures on your property:
- Detached garage or shed
- Fence
- Gazebo
- Swimming pool

PERSONAL PROPERTY (Coverage C)
Limit: $175,000 (50% of Coverage A)
Covers your belongings including:
- Furniture and electronics
- Clothing and jewelry
- Appliances
- Tools and equipment

Special limits apply to:
- Jewelry: $2,500 total
- Firearms: $2,500 total
- Cash and coins: $500
- Securities: $1,500
- Watercraft: $1,500

Schedule high-value items separately for full coverage.

LOSS OF USE (Coverage D)
Limit: $70,000 (20% of Coverage A)
If your home becomes uninhabitable, covers:
- Hotel or rental costs
- Additional living expenses
- Food costs exceeding normal expenses
Maximum duration: 24 months

PERSONAL LIABILITY (Coverage E)
Limit: $500,000 per occurrence
Protects you if someone is injured on your property or you damage someone else's property.
Includes legal defense costs.

MEDICAL PAYMENTS (Coverage F)
Limit: $5,000 per person
Covers medical expenses for guests injured on your property, regardless of fault.

COVERED PERILS
Standard Form (HO-3) covers dwelling from:
- Fire and lightning
- Windstorm and hail
- Explosion
- Theft and vandalism
- Water damage from plumbing/appliance failure
- Weight of ice, snow, or sleet
- Falling objects
- Volcanic eruption

Personal property covered on named-perils basis.

EXCLUSIONS
Not covered:
- Flood damage (requires separate flood insurance)
- Earthquake (requires separate earthquake insurance)
- Sewer backup (unless endorsement purchased)
- Maintenance issues and wear and tear
- Intentional damage
- War or nuclear hazard
- Mold (limited coverage, may require endorsement)

DEDUCTIBLES
All perils: $1,000 deductible
Hurricane/Named storm: 2% of dwelling coverage ($7,000)

CLAIMS PROCESS
1. Contact us immediately to report loss
2. Mitigate further damage (emergency repairs covered)
3. Document damage with photos/videos
4. Save damaged items for inspection
5. Provide receipts for emergency expenses
6. Adjuster will inspect within 48 hours

ADDITIONAL COVERAGES (Included)
- Debris removal: Up to 5% of Coverage A
- Fire department service charge: $500
- Trees and shrubs: $5,000 total, $500 per item (limited perils)
- Credit card fraud: $500
- Lock replacement after theft: Actual cost

OPTIONAL ENDORSEMENTS
- Replacement cost on contents: +$150/year
- Water backup coverage: +$75/year
- Scheduled personal property: Varies by item value
- Increased jewelry/firearms limits: Varies
- Home business coverage: Varies
- Earthquake coverage: Based on location
- Umbrella liability: $1M for +$200/year

PREMIUM FACTORS
Your premium is based on:
- Home age and construction type
- Square footage and number of stories
- Roof age and material
- Distance to fire station
- Home security features
- Claims history
- Credit-based insurance score
- Deductible selection

DISCOUNTS APPLIED
- Home security system: 10%
- Fire/smoke alarms: 5%
- Storm shutters: 5%
- Multi-policy (bundled with auto): 15%
- Claims-free for 5 years: 10%

POLICY REQUIREMENTS
You must:
- Maintain the property in good condition
- Notify us of property improvements exceeding $10,000
- Report changes in occupancy or use
- Maintain minimum security standards
- Report claims promptly

RENEWAL TERMS
Policy renews annually. Premium may change based on:
- Claims filed during term
- Changes in replacement cost
- Updates to risk factors
- State-approved rate changes

30 days notice required for non-renewal or cancellation.
