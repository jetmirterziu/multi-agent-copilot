AUTO INSURANCE POLICY DOCUMENT
Policy Number: AUTO-2024-001
Effective Date: January 1, 2024

COVERAGE SUMMARY
This comprehensive auto insurance policy provides coverage for personal vehicles with the following components:

LIABILITY COVERAGE
Bodily Injury Liability: $250,000 per person / $500,000 per accident
Property Damage Liability: $100,000 per accident
This coverage protects you if you're legally responsible for injuries or property damage to others in an accident.

COLLISION COVERAGE
Deductible: $500
Covers damage to your vehicle from collisions with other vehicles or objects, regardless of fault.

COMPREHENSIVE COVERAGE
Deductible: $250
Covers damage to your vehicle from non-collision events including:
- Theft
- Vandalism
- Fire
- Weather damage (hail, flood, wind)
- Animal strikes

MEDICAL PAYMENTS COVERAGE
Limit: $5,000 per person
Covers medical expenses for you and your passengers regardless of fault.

UNINSURED/UNDERINSURED MOTORIST COVERAGE
Bodily Injury: $250,000 per person / $500,000 per accident
Property Damage: $50,000 per accident
Protects you if you're in an accident with a driver who has no insurance or insufficient insurance.

CLAIMS PROCESS
1. Report the claim within 24 hours of the incident
2. Provide police report number if applicable
3. Submit photographs of damage
4. Obtain repair estimates from approved vendors
5. Claims are processed within 5-7 business days

POLICY EXCLUSIONS
- Intentional damage
- Racing or competitive events
- Commercial use without proper endorsement
- Driving under the influence
- Wear and tear

PREMIUM CALCULATION FACTORS
Premiums are based on:
- Driver age and experience
- Driving record (accidents and violations)
- Vehicle make, model, and year
- Annual mileage
- Geographic location
- Credit score

DISCOUNTS AVAILABLE
- Safe driver discount: 15% (no accidents for 3 years)
- Multi-vehicle discount: 10%
- Good student discount: 10% (grade B or higher)
- Defensive driving course: 5%
- Anti-theft device: 5%

RENEWAL AND CANCELLATION
Policy renews automatically unless cancelled with 30 days notice. Mid-term cancellation may result in short-rate penalty.
