COVERAGE PROVISIONS

When Coverage Begins:
Coverage effective on policy date after:
- Application approval
- First premium payment received
- Medical examination completed (if required)
- No adverse health changes since application

Free Look Period:
30-day free look period from receipt of policy. May cancel for full premium refund if returned within 30 days.

Suicide Exclusion:
No death benefit paid if insured commits suicide within 2 years of policy effective date. Premiums will be refunded.

Contestability Period:
Insurer may contest coverage within 2 years of effective date if material misrepresentation discovered on application.

POLICY OPTIONS AND RIDERS

Accelerated Death Benefit Rider (Included):
May access up to 75% of death benefit if diagnosed with terminal illness (life expectancy <12 months). Reduces death benefit by amount accessed plus interest.

Waiver of Premium Rider (Optional - $45/year):
Waives premiums if insured becomes totally disabled for 6+ months before age 60. Must provide proof of disability.

Child Term Rider (Optional - $65/year):
Covers all dependent children. $10,000 coverage per child until age 25.

Accidental Death Benefit Rider (Optional - $85/year):
Pays additional $500,000 if death results from accident. Not available after age 70.

CONVERSION PRIVILEGE

Conversion options:

POLICY LAPSE AND REINSTATEMENT

Grace Period:
31-day grace period for premium payments. Coverage continues during grace period.

Policy Lapse:
Policy lapses if premium not received within grace period. All coverage terminates immediately.

Reinstatement:
May apply to reinstate lapsed policy within 3 years if:
- Pay all past-due premiums plus interest
- Provide evidence of insurability
- Complete health questionnaire
- May require new medical exam

RENEWAL AND CONVERSION AT TERM END

At end of 20-year term, options include:

3. Convert to permanent insurance (if within conversion period)

CLAIMS PROCESS

To file death claim, beneficiary must:
1. Contact claims department at 1-800-CLAIMS-1
2. Provide certified death certificate
3. Complete claimant statement form
4. Provide proof of beneficiary identity
5. Submit any additional requested documentation

Typical claim processing:
- Acknowledgment: Within 5 business days
- Processing time: 15-30 days after all documents received
- Payment: Check or electronic transfer

Claim delays may occur if:
- Death within 2-year contestability period
- Suspicious circumstances
- Beneficiary disputes
- Missing documentation

EXCLUSIONS AND LIMITATIONS

Death benefit not payable if death results from:
- Suicide within 2 years of effective date
- War or act of war
- Aviation (except fare-paying passenger)
- Illegal activities
- Intentional self-inflicted injury

Medical Exam Requirements:
- Under $250,000: Health questionnaire only
- $250,000-$500,000: Exam, blood, urine test
- $500,000-$1,000,000: Full exam, EKG, additional tests
- Over $1,000,000: Comprehensive underwriting, financial review
